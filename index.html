Meet Your Therapist

A 5-Star Salon Built on Results, Not Gimmicks

Cloud Nine Beauty is a boutique beauty salon in the heart of Coleraine, run by Angela — a passionate, experienced beauty therapist who believes every woman deserves to feel confident in her own skin.

This isn't a production line. It's a bright, comfortable, welcoming space where you get genuine one-on-one care, honest advice, and treatments tailored to your skin and your goals. Whether you're battling breakouts, want that lit-from-within glow, or just need an hour of pure pampering — you're in the right place.

We use only professional-grade products from AlumierMD, Germaine de Capuccini, and Jane Iredale — because your skin deserves better than off-the-shelf.
